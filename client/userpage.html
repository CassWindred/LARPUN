User Guide

Use the blue buttons in order to submit certain actions, their cost is described in the labels to the right of each description. For example, pressing the "Study" button will expend 2 Time Units in order to gain 20 research points.

Complex actions work in the same way. Fill in the text box with a description of your action, and then press the submit button, this will expend the actions cost and submit your action to the referees.
